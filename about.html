About Crave.ai

Crave.ai is a locally-run AI chatbot builder platform that allows you to create, customize, and chat with AI companions tailored to your preferences. Whether you're looking for a friendly conversation partner, a romantic interest, or something more adventurous, Crave.ai gives you the tools to build your fantasy and chat your way.

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, private, and completely free to use. With no login required and all data stored locally on your device, you can create unlimited chatbots and have unlimited conversations without any restrictions.

How It Works

Crave.ai runs entirely in your browser. All chatbot data, including personalities, images, and conversation history, is stored in your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any server unless you choose to use the OpenAI integration.

The AI responses can be generated in two ways:

  1. Local Generation: Without an API key, responses are generated based on the chatbot's personality, tone, and category settings using predefined patterns.
  2. OpenAI Integration: With an API key (set in Settings), responses are generated using OpenAI's powerful language models, creating more dynamic and intelligent conversations.

Your OpenAI API key, if provided, is stored securely in your browser's local storage and is only used to make requests to OpenAI's servers for generating responses.
